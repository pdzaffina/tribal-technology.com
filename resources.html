RFP Addendum: Data & AI Protection Requirements

When a tribal nation issues an RFP for software, platforms, or AI-enabled services, most standard templates don't ask the right questions about data ownership, AI transparency, or sovereign authority. This addendum fills that gap.

Attach it to any technology RFP and require vendors to respond in writing. Weak or vague answers are themselves a signal. The questions cover five protection domains and include red-flag answer guides so your procurement team knows what to watch for.

What's inside
  • Section 1 — Data Ownership & Control (4 questions)
  • Section 2 — AI & Automated Decision-Making (5 questions)
  • Section 3 — Data Sharing & Third Parties (3 questions)
  • Section 4 — Security & Breach Response (3 questions)
  • Section 5 — Sovereignty & Jurisdiction (3 questions)
  • Red-flag answer guide for each question
  • Instructions for incorporating responses into the final contract
